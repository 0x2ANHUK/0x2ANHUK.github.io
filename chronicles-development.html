💡 WHAT WE LEARNED

The Insights

  • Constraints breed creativity. Limited to text, ASCII artists created masterpieces. Limited to 6 commits, we built a narrative.
  • Local assets > CDN. 40MB of GIFs self-hosted beats flaky external services.
  • Narrative > Marketing. "Based on real research" resonates more than "✓ Mobile responsive."
  • Mobile-first is mandatory. 320px or 2560px—it all needs to work.
  • Memes are documentation. A single Drake meme conveys more than a paragraph of text.
  • Animations should be purpose-driven. Glow effects should enhance, not distract.
  • Git history is a story. Commits are chapters. Messages are meaning.
